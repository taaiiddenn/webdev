one of japans biggest pieces of culture is its food. Japanese food is known for its incredible flavors and beautiful presentation. One popular dish is sushi, which combines rice with fresh fish or vegetables. Ramen is a comforting noodle soup with various toppings like pork and eggs. Tempura is another favorite, featuring lightly battered and fried seafood or vegetables. Kaiseki is a multi-course meal that showcases seasonal ingredients in an artful way.
